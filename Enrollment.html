 Enrollment Notes:  
 报名须知： 

1. Course Consultation课程咨询
Please contact our staff to understand the course details and confirm which course suits your learning needs.
请先与我们的工作人员联系，了解课程内容并确认自身的学习需求与适合的课程类型。

2. Choose a Teacher & Schedule选择教师与上课时段
Select a suitable teacher and confirm your preferred class time.
根据您的学习目标与时间安排，选择合适的教师并确定上课时段。

3. Prepaid Tuition预付学费
You need to prepay the tuition. The minimum purchase is 10 class hours, valid for 3 months.
需提前预付学费，最低预付为 10 个课时，有效期为 三个月。

4. Confirm the First Lesson确认首次上课时间
After registration and payment, please contact the teacher to confirm your first class time. (A fixed weekly class schedule is recommended.)
完成报名和缴费后，请与任课老师联系，双方确认首次上课时间。（建议每周固定上课时间，便于持续学习。）



 Reminder:

If you need to reschedule, please inform the teacher at least 48 hours in advance.

若未能及时通知，将按照原定课时正常计费，敬请理解。
