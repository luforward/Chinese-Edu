LuForward Chinese 陆孚中文 is a Singapore-based local company dedicated to providing high-quality online Chinese language courses for adult learners in Singapore.

All our teachers are native Chinese speakers with a strong linguistic foundation and solid bilingual abilities in both Chinese and English. We have years of experience in the education sector or relevant professional fields. At the same time, we are familiar with Singapore’s education system, local culture, and workplace environment, enabling us to design and deliver localized Chinese courses tailored to the needs of learners in Singapore.
